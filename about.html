About me

Hello! I'm Alexis Vilaça. I'm a motion designer living in Portugal.

I discovered the passion for motion design at the age of 17 and since then I've been doing work in this industry and thriving on learning new skills and developing my portfolio.

I finished high school as a 'Multimedia' student and during that time I interned at Gen Design Studio as a motion designer. After high school, I began working in the industry as a Graphic Designer, for a little over a year, then I made the transition to Pixartidea to work as a motion designer. I'm currently working at WIT Software as a motion designer.

I manage my day job with personal work, as a way of improving and learn new skills that will help me progress in the industry and ultimately see how my work evolved along that time. You can check my personal work here.

I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities that will help me grow as a motion designer. I'm currently available for remote freelance opportunities. Feel free to reach out.
