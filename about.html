About

My name is Alexis Vilaça and I´m a Motion Designer living in Portugal.

I discovered the interest for Motion Design at the age of 17 and since then I´ve been doing work in this field and thriving on learning new skills and developing my portfolio. My work is mainly focused on 2D and 3D animation.

Early last year I challenged myself to create a piece of artwork on a regular basis to improve and learn new skills that will help me progress in the industry and ultimately see how my work evolved along that time. You can look it up here.
