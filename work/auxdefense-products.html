AuxDefense Products

AuxDefense is a project funded by the Ministry of National Defense with the purpose of developing advanced personal protection equipment. With this project is expected the creation of innovative products such as bulletproof helmets and vests and combat clothing, including knee and elbow pads.

The purpose of this video was to show the development phases of the products and then present the three products showing the characteristics and elements of each.
