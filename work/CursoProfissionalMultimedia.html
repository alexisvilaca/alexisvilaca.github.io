Curso Profissional de Multimédia

Explainer Video

The professional multimedia course at Externato Infante D. Henrique is intended to students from 10th to 12th grade. In the course the students will explore a practical education and learn the areas of photography, video production, graphic design, animation and web development.

This promotional video intended to introduce and promote the course by explaining what the students learn, the prizes won in the contests they participate in, their equipment and what the students can do after the course.
