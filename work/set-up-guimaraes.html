Set.Up Guimarães — Explainer

Set.Up Guimarães is a "super incubator" strategically located in one of Portugal´s most industrialized regions to promote local entrepreneurship and innovative ideas, engaging in three areas of knowledge: creative (LabPac), industrial (PevFactory) and technological (TecPark).

The goal was to create a communication tool, through an explainer video, introducing the Set.Up Guimarães brand and its three areas that comprise it.
