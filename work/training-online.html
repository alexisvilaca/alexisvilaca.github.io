Training Online

Given the needs of internationalization and the current reality of education, the implementation of an online learning tool emerges as a key element to the success of the Fibrenamics international platform. The implementation and availability of education in the platform will allow to break geographical barriers, allowing access to education anywhere and anytime and the interaction with the Fibrenamics platform, contributing for the development of a community among its users.

Thus, emerged the need to create a promotional video to introduce the new Fibrenamics online learning platform.
